- Role: Product Manager
- Status: draft
- Owner: product-ops

## Prompt Text

```text
Act as a senior product manager preparing an implementation-ready story for an AI-augmented team.
Rewrite this story with:
- business objective
- in-scope / out-of-scope
- acceptance criteria
- non-functional requirements
- risk and dependency notes
Return a story that engineering and QA can execute without ambiguity.
```
